Research

1.         Integrated circuit design:

1) A new self-powered P-SSHI circuit for  piezoelectric energy harvesting

The research explores analytical models and new integrated interface circuit design for piezoelectric energy harvesting. Both a MOSFET-based circuit and a BJT-based circuit were implemented for a self-powered P-SSHI rectifier. The circuits were analysed by simulation and showed higher power output in comparison with former ones.

2) CMOS Integrated circuit for physical unclonable function (PUF)

This research explores a Physical Unclonable Function (PUF) in integrated circuit with higher robustness against noises. Differential amplifier is utilized and noises (power supply noises and ground noises) are eliminated as common-mode input. The high performance is proved by noise analysis and Monte Carlo simulation. 
2.        Sensors & systems:

1) Implementation of a simple electrocardiograph(ECG)>>see details

I conducted my own study to implement a helpful practical healthcare product, ECG. The system was built using DC regulated power supply, INA, Bessel filter and MCU-based programming. It is a helpful healthcare product with good performance, though simple!

2) Eddy-current sensor for blade-tip clearance detection

I was mainly responsible for the signal-processing circuit in this research. The equivalent circuit model of the sensor was explored and the optimized shape of the head was obtained via simulation and controlled experiments. A host computer controlling interface based on LabView was also built.

3) Circuit-experiment platform with a CompactRIO-based remote control (on progress)

This is a full experience of mathematical analysis, circuit design, simulation and Labview programming. We explore an experimental system which will be extended to many other universities in China. It consists of a rebalanced loop of DTG, involving a DTG simulator, a series of signal-processing circuits and a compactRIO remote control system based on Labview. 

3.        Algorithms

1) Vision-based Surface Measurement based on Focus Variation Technology


I conducted my own study to explore dual-tree complex wavelet transform (DTCWT) for a new focusfunction. It takes advantage of the better direction selectivity of DTCWT. Detail procedures of focus variation method was also explored by programming on MATLAB and verified by measuring a MEMS structure.

2)White-light Interferometer based on Multi-wavelength Analysis

This research explores the use of Hilbert transform in phase solution fringe order method in height resolution.  It is an experience of programming on MATLAB, dealing with simulated and acquired images
