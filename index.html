Biography

 

Shimiao Li is currently a senior student in School of Precision Instrument & Opto-Electronics Engineering in Tianjin University, China. She is studying for a bachelor degree with excellent GPA and English. Outside lesson times, she is working closely with Prof Tong Guo and Prof. Xingfei Li.

 

She has done some research in the State key laboratory of precision measurement technology and instruments, where she works with Prof. Tong Guo.            

 

She started her research

 

For her PhD study, she is mostly interested in the following major directions:

      1) apply knowledge of circuits to building up sensors and systems that provide various functions.

      2) develop new algorithms for integrated circuits and systems

      3) explore the application of optics in circuitry
